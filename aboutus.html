About Us

ABOUT US

Ansh Econest is a Hyderabad-based manufacturing company dedicated to creating eco-friendly, biodegradable, and paper-based disposable products and sustainable packaging solutions. Founded in 2023, we specialize in compostable food containers, recyclable materials, and restaurant disposables that support zero-waste lifestyles. As a responsible Indian eco-product manufacturer, our mission is rooted in sustainability, ethical manufacturing, and promote eco-conscious innovation. With a vision for a cleaner planet and a circular economy, Ansh Econest is committed to providing earth-friendly choices that empower climate-conscious businesses across India.
