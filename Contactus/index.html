FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of paper cup blanks do you manufacture?

We manufacture a variety of paper cup blanks including single-wall, double-wall, and ripple-wall cup blanks in various sizes and capacities to meet different customer needs.

What materials are used in your paper cup blanks?

Our paper cup blanks are made from high-quality, food-grade paper with a PE (polyethylene) or PLA (polylactic acid) coating to ensure durability and safety for hot and cold beverages.

Do you provide samples?

Yes, we offer samples for quality assurance. Shipping charges may apply for samples.

Can I customize the design or printing?

Yes, we provide customized printing on the blanks. You can share your logo, artwork, or design, and we’ll print it with high-quality, eco-friendly inks.

Do you offer custom printing on paper cup blanks?

Yes, we offer custom printing services for our paper cup blanks. You can provide your own design or work with our design team to create the perfect look for your brand.
