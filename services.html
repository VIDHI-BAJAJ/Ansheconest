Our Services

Coated Base Paper

Our high-performance coated base paper is ideal for disposable products, eco-friendly packaging, and premium print media. Available with PE (Polyethylene) coating for oil and water resistance, heat sealability, and leakproof properties, WBHS (Water-Based Heat Sealable) for an eco-conscious option, and PLA (Polylactic Acid) — a biodegradable, plant-based thermoplastic with all the above benefits. We offer Clay Coated White Board (CCWB) with PE, WBHS, or PLA coatings, delivering a smooth surface and excellent printability. For the food and beverage industry, our White Cup Stock Board is perfect for paper cups.

Paper Blanks

We offer high-quality paper cup blanks in all standard sizes, ranging from 45ml to 300ml and 12oz to 22oz, suitable for a wide range of beverage and food applications. Our paper blanks are manufactured to meet international quality standards, ensuring durability, printability, and leak resistance. We also provide ripple paper cup blanks for enhanced insulation and heat protection, ideal for hot drinks. Whether you need custom paper cup blanks or standard sizes, we deliver precision-cut solutions for reliable cup manufacturing. Choose our blanks for premium disposable paper cups that meet environmental expectations.

Disposable Food Containers

Our range of disposable food containers includes soup bowls (250ml, 350ml, 550ml, 750ml) and salad bowls (750ml, 1000ml, 1250ml), made from certified high-quality white paper board. These eco-friendly containers are water- and grease-resistant, making them perfect for hot soups, curries, and cold dishes like salads and pasta. Ideal for both dine-in and delivery, they combine premium appearance with reliable performance. Whether you need takeaway containers or sustainable food packaging, our bowls offer durability, moisture resistance, and style in every serving.

Kraft Paper Containers

Our Kraft paper containers are available in natural brown Kraft and dual-tone (brown outside, white inside) finishes, offering a perfect blend of durability and style. Made from certified double-sided Kraft board, they are fully water- and grease-resistant, ideal for hot or cold dishes, desserts, and fast food. The brown Kraft containers provide a sturdy, rustic look that enhances shelf appeal and ensures leak-free delivery. The white-inside Kraft variant offers a premium, refined touch, making it ideal for upscale foodservice and takeaway brands. Paper lids are exclusively available for a complete sustainable packaging solution.

Paper Cup Single Wall

We offer a versatile range of single wall paper cups in various sizes to meet every serving need. Our small-size cups (65ml, 75ml, 85ml, 90ml) are perfect for espresso and small beverages, suitable for both hot and cold drinks. Medium-size cups come in long and wide formats — 100ml and 120ml long cups offer a tall, sleek profile, while 120ml and 150ml wide cups provide greater stability for larger servings. For bigger needs, our large-size paper cups (210ml, 240ml, 300ml) are ideal for juices, sodas, and cold drinks, designed for leak resistance and durability. We also offer ice cream cups in two 100ml variants.

Ripple Cups and Double Wall Cups

Our ripple paper cups are available in sizes 120ml, 150ml, 210ml, 240ml, and 12oz, designed specifically for hot beverages like coffee and tea. Featuring a dual-layer corrugated design, these cups provide superior heat insulation, enhanced grip, and excellent structural strength—perfect for on-the-go use. For cold drinks, our double wall paper cups come in 210ml, 240ml, 12oz, 16oz, and 22oz sizes. Built with an extra paper layer, they offer moisture resistance, prevent softening, and maintain durability and comfort, making them ideal for chilled beverages in takeaways, cafes, and events.
